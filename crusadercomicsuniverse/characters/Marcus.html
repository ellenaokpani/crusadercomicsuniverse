Marcus Taylor

About:

Athletic body build - Marcus is a proud airforce man so he wears his uniform every time he goes out.

Stats:
  • Age: 26
  • Height: 6"1
  • Weight: 210lbs
  • Characteristics: Marcus is headstrong, confident, playful
  • Quirk: Marcus tends to make video game references in conversations.
  • Flaws:Marcus tries to make light of every situation he encounters. Marcus expects his fellow African Americans to do better and escape the norm.
Background:

Marcus was born in the heart of Chicago during the worst time it has ever been. Marcus seen people that look just like fight, steal and kill each other on a daily basis, so Marcus escapes to playing video games. Marcus' parents moved him to Atlanta Georgia where he met Luke Laster. Later in life both Marcus and Luke join the military. Marcus climbed the ranks and was offered an opportunity to go into space. Now in space Marcus has a new purpose to chase after.
